Judge Checklist

Recommended review order

1

Check the public landing page, featured metrics, and overall visual presentation.

2

Open Events and inspect live registration behavior, including optional participant email collection, event limits, and registration open/close states.

3

Review Resources, Leaderboard, FAQ, Contact, and About for information flow and usability.

4

Open the admin login using the direct shortcut above or by double-clicking the RIT logo, then sign in with the provided judge credentials.

5

Inside admin, verify event creation, registration settings, participant list generation, OD review, and resource management.

6

Use generated participant lists, CSV/PDF export, and gatekeeper check-in to evaluate backend utility for organizers on event day.
