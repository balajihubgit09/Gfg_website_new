About GFG Club

A student coding community focused on learning, building, and growing together.

GFG Club brings students together through coding practice, workshops, events, and peer learning. The goal is simple: help students become better problem solvers and stronger builders.

Mission

What we do

We create a practical space for students to improve coding skills, collaborate on ideas, and stay ready for contests, events, and interviews.

Vision

Where we are going

We want to build a strong campus tech culture where students consistently learn, innovate, and contribute to meaningful technical work.

Community

How we grow

Through team participation, regular events, resource sharing, and student-led support, members grow faster together than alone.

Club Activities

What members can expect

Coding contests Workshops Project building Interview preparation Team collaboration Technical sessions
